Vision

Comake is revolutionizing democracy. We are bringing ultimate transparency, accountability and ownership to how taxpayer money is spent at a local level. We allow taxpayers to decide how their local taxes are spent by giving them the ability to allocate funding to issues they care about or problems they want solved.

Problems We're Solving

  • Government mismanagement of tax dollars
  • Taxpayers don't know how their local taxes are spent
  • Taxpayers feel frustrated that issues they care about lack funding
